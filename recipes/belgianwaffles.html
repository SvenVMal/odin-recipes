Belgian Waffels

Description

Belgian Waffles are iconic treats known for their distinctive grid pattern, crisp exterior, and fluffy interior. Cooked to golden perfection, these waffles offer a delightful canvas for toppings like whipped cream, fresh fruits, and syrup. They're enjoyed as both a sweet breakfast indulgence and a delectable dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream, fresh berries, or chocolate for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your waffle iron according to its instructions.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs and then add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. The batter may be slightly lumpy.
  5. Ladle the batter onto the preheated waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer's instructions until the waffles are golden and crisp.
  6. Serve the waffles warm, topped with whipped cream, fresh berries, or a drizzle of chocolate if desired.
